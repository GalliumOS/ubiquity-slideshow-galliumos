Want to Help?

If you would like to help us improve GalliumOS, please get in touch!

You don't have to be a developer — there are many different kinds of projects that contribute to building a full Linux distribution for Chromebooks and Chromeboxes.

Join us on Reddit or IRC to find out what's on the current to-do list. It's never empty!
